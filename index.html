The ECF.network Platform

The platform implements the Pensieve Mechanism to enable more efficient coordination and to extract collective intelligence from the community for the promotion of accountable and legitimate legitimacy Blockchain & Web3 web 3 projects across the entire spectrum of the crypto space

Below is a small preview of how we apply these ideas that we are currently building

1 2 3 4 5 / 5

  • Project Proposals

    Create proposals for new projects to be added to the platform. A blueprint of essential items that must be provided is included in a proposal

    After submitting a proposal, the community can vote on the entries provided for each item. Once a proposal has reached max support, it is then published as the official project page on ECF.network

  • Community Verification

    Each item has a weight that must be exceeded and a minimum of unique votes per item (quorum) in order to pass

    Once a proposal has exceeded all item weights and achieved quorum, it has won, and the proposed project becomes published in the main project index and is assigned its own page

  • Gain Reputation

    Both the creator of a proposed project and the winning proposal are rewarded

    If the proposer of the project in addition has their first proposal therein wins, they get both the Bounty and the Zero-to-one Rewards

  • Project Pages

    Projects contain a profile view of community-verified data that can be linked in real-time to a Blockchain, or other sources

    More items added to a project page by the community improve its overall credibility and raise its ranking in the main index

    The goal is to incentivize the community to verify and own the information in an open and credible way

  • Propose Items & Edits

    Project pages also contain a contribution view where users can, as when proposing new projects, propose new items to be added to a project page or update existing items by submitting their own entries

    Proposed item additions and update submissions are all subject to dynamic voting by the community
